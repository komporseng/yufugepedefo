He participated for the first time in the American Ninja Warrior in season 3 and keep participating until season 11. He is also known as the Real Life Ninja in the United States for his incredible performances in those events.

He is also the first participant who has won the title Trifecta in season 11 of the American Ninja Warrior championship. He is the only participant who has completed cargo climb, spider climb, invisible ladder, elevator climb, and spider trap in city finals.

Apart from these achievements, Drew has completed various other achievements in his career like the only participant to complete three versions of Sidewinder, to complete Ultra Crazy Cliffhanger, and several others.
