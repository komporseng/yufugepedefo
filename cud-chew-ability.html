Cud Chew

Flavor text

Generation IX
When the Pokémon eats a Berry, it will regurgitate that Berry at the end of the next turn and eat it one more time.

Cud Chew (Japanese: はんすう Cud Chew) is an Ability introduced in Generation IX.

Effect

In battle

This move effect may be in need of research.
Reason: Version 1.3 of SV corrected a glitch where Cud Chew would activate every other turn.
You can discuss this on the talk page.

If a Pokémon with Cud Chew eats a Berry or is affected by a Flung Berry, its effect will activate again at the end of the next turn.
