Brandi Glanville joins Amber Portwood for Marriage Boot Camp Reality Stars Family Edition

Former The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star Brandi Glanville will reportedly be featured on the upcoming season of Marriage Boot Camp Reality Stars: Family Edition alongside Teen Mom OG‘s Amber Portwood. Hmmmmm… could Brandi be the “blonde jackass” and/or “drunk bimbo” that Amber was tweeting about last week?!

Back to the Brandi news… Page Six was the first to report that Brandi will be one of the houseguests this season, and they revealed just what “family” Brandi was bringing along — her dad, Guy Glanville.

That explains this next photo of Brandi with her dad, which she shared just two-and-a-half weeks ago, which would have been just as the cast were entering the house to begin filming:

“My Father’s Day date is… Guy Glanville!” Brandi captioned the picture. “Enjoy ur day dads! Love this man ❤️❤️(most of the time ;))!” It’s unclear exactly what issues Brandi will be trying to work through with her dad. (ROBH fans will recall that he has had some serious medical issues over the last few years, requiring multiple surgeries.)
