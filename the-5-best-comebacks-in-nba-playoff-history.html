The 5 best comebacks in NBA playoff history

The NBA playoffs have witnessed some of the most breathtaking and unforgettable moments in basketball history. From epic comebacks to thrilling finishes, the playoffs have provided fans with an abundance of excitement. 

1. Cleveland Cavaliers vs. Golden State Warriors - 2016 NBA Finals

The 2016 NBA Finals stand as one of the greatest comebacks in NBA history. The Cleveland Cavaliers, led by LeBron James and Kyrie Irving, defied the odds when they overcame a 3-1 series deficit against the Golden State Warriors.

Despite facing a formidable Warriors team that had finished a record-breaking season, the Cavaliers staged a remarkable turnaround to claim the championship.

LeBron and Kyrie's heroics propelled the Cavs to a historic 4-3 series victory, marking the team's first professional sporting trophy in 50 years.

2. LA Clippers vs. Memphis Grizzlies - 2012 Western Conference First Round

The LA Clippers etched their name in NBA playoff lore with an extraordinary 27-point comeback against the Memphis Grizzlies in Game 1 of the 2012 Western Conference First Round. Down by 27 late in the third quarter, the Clippers mounted a sensational rally, culminating in a thrilling 99-98 victory. 

3. Boston Celtics vs. New Jersey Nets - 2002 Eastern Conference Finals

In 2002, the Boston Celtics staged the greatest fourth-quarter comeback in NBA playoff history against the New Jersey Nets. Trailing by 26 points heading into the final quarter, the Celtics orchestrated a dramatic turnaround, outscoring the Nets 41-16 to secure a remarkable 94-90 victory.

Paul Pierce's heroic performance, where he scored 36 points and had 11 assists, propelled the Celtics to an unforgettable comeback win.

4. LA Lakers vs. Seattle SuperSonics - 1989 Western Conference Semifinals

The Los Angeles Lakers' legendary comeback against the Seattle SuperSonics in the 1989 Western Conference Semifinals ranks as one of the most astonishing turnarounds in NBA history. Facing a daunting 29-point deficit, the Lakers, led by Magic Johnson, orchestrated a stunning comeback, ultimately securing a thrilling 97-95 victory. 

5.Atlanta Hawks vs. Philadelphia 76ers - 2021 Eastern Conference Semifinals

The Atlanta Hawks' remarkable 26-point comeback against the Philadelphia 76ers in the 2021 Eastern Conference Semifinals showcased the resilience and tenacity of the Hawks. Overcoming a substantial deficit, the Hawks staged a remarkable rally, ultimately clinching a thrilling 109-106 victory.

This unforgettable comeback victory solidified the Hawks' place in NBA playoff history, highlighting the captivating drama that defines the playoffs.

 

The NBA playoffs have produced countless unforgettable moments, and these five comebacks stand out as the most extraordinary in the history of the league.

From historic series turnarounds to sensational come-from-behind victories, these moments exemplify the resilience, skill, and determination that define the essence of playoff basketball.

The postseason is a stage where the extraordinary becomes the norm, and these remarkable comebacks have etched themselves into the annals of basketball history, captivating fans and players alike with their unyielding drama and unforgettable narratives.
