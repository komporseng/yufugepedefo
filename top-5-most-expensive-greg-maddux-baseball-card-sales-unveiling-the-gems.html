Top 5 Most Expensive Greg Maddux Baseball Card Sales: Unveiling the Gems

One of the greatest hurlers to ever take the mound, famed for his pinpoint accuracy and command in Major League Baseball. But did you realize that his rookie cards have become popular with sports card investors? In this article, we count down Greg Maddux's top 5 most valuable baseball cards fetching four figures or more.

 

Unveiling the Gems: Greg Maddux's Most Expensive Baseball Cards

1. 1987 Leaf Greg Maddux Rookie Card #36

  • Highest Sale Price: $3,250
  • Supply: 498
  • Brand: Leaf The 1987 Leaf Greg Maddux rookie card #36 stands out as one of his most iconic cards. With its distinctive black borders, this card has gained significant value over time. Depending on its condition, this prized find for collectors can fetch it for an impressive $2,150. However, collectors need to be cautious of the delicate black borders, which can easily scuff.

2. 1987 Donruss Greg Maddux Rookie Card #52

  • Highest Sale Price: $570
  • Supply: 468
  • Brand: Donruss Rookies Although not his true rookie card, the 1987 Donruss Rookies Greg Maddux #52 card is highly sought-after. It's a tougher find than his regular season cards and is distinguishable by its glossy finish and distinctive green borders. 

3. 1987 Fleer Update Greg Maddux Rookie Card #U68

  • Highest Sale Price: $700
  • Supply: 817
  • Brand: Fleer Update The 1987 Fleer Update Greg Maddux rookie card #U68 is an affordable yet valuable find for collectors. Featuring a cool blue and white border, captures Maddux in his post-Chicago Cubs days. Its premium glossy finish adds to its appeal, making it a gem in any collection.

4. 1987 Topps Traded Tiffany Greg Maddux Rookie Card

  • Sale Price: $600 (PSA 10) The 1987 Topps Traded Tiffany Greg Maddux rookie card, graded PSA 10, scored an impressive $600 on eBay. This card, known for its investment potential, is a testament to the surging sports card investment market, where enthusiasts are willing to shell out significant amounts for prized cards.

5. 1999 Metal Universe Precious Metal Gems #215 Greg Maddux

  • Estimated Worth: $3999.99 The most valuable card of Greg Maddux, the 1999 Metal Universe Precious Metal Gems #215, holds an estimated worth of approximately $3999.99. This card is truly a gem in the world of sports card collecting, and its value reflects the unparalleled legacy of the iconic pitcher.

How to Find These Gems

Collectors should be on the lookout to find these gems in Greg Maddux's rookie card. Cards like these have sold for high values on online platforms such as eBay, thus indicating their investment potential. Conditions, rarity, and autograph status all play into the pricing of the card. These cards are a major lottery ticket for any collector with the market rising as quickly as we see in sports card investing.

Add in the legendary pitching pedigree and investment potential, these certainly bring out the collector/enthusiast among us all. As the sports card investment market continues to expending, these cards are shining stars of long-time past and allure for a significant number of amateur enthusiasts.
