Top 5 Most Expensive Calvin Johnson Football Cards

Calvin Johnson, the former Detroit Lion wide receiver also known as "Megatron," had a profound legacy during his time in football. Not only was he king of the field, but he also became one in football card collecting. Here, we will break down his 5 most expensive football cards.

1. 2007 Finest Rookie Autographs Blue Xfractors #135 Calvin Johnson /50 BGS 9.5

  • Sale Price: Approximately $1700.00 This card stands out as the most valuable in the collection due to its scarcity and pristine condition. With only 50 copies in existence, it's no wonder that this card commands such a high price.

2. 2007 Exquisite Collection Calvin Johnson RC #130 Autograph Jersey #/99

  • Sale Price: 1,825. This autographed relic rookie card, limited to just 99 copies, is a prized possession among collectors. Its rarity and appealing design elevate its value, making it one of the most sought-after cards featuring Calvin Johnson.

3. 2007 Playoff National Treasures Calvin Johnson Auto RC #107 Autograph Jersey #/99

  • Sale Price: $1450. The autographed relic rookie card from the National Treasures series continues to captivate collectors due to its limited edition and exquisite design. Its scarcity and association with a premier collection contribute to its high value.

4. 2007 Topps Chrome Calvin Johnson RC #TC200 Red Refractor /139

  • Sale Price: Around $1000.00 This Red Refractor edition of the Topps Chrome card is a highly sought-after gem. With only 139 copies in existence, this rare find is a testament to Calvin Johnson's enduring legacy and the fervor of football card enthusiasts.

5. 2007 Topps Chrome White Refractor Rookie Card #TC200 Auto PSA 10

  • Sale Price: Approximately $899.00 This autographed White Refractor edition from the Topps Chrome series boasts a pristine PSA 10 grade, adding to its allure and value. Its scarcity and pristine condition make it a coveted addition to any collection.

In the End

In conclusion, Calvin Johnson's impact on the football field transcends his playing days, as evidenced by the enduring allure of his football cards. The top 5 most expensive Calvin Johnson football cards stand as a testament to his enduring legacy and the fervor of football card collectors worldwide. As enthusiasts continue to seek out these prized possessions, the thrill of the hunt adds an extra dimension to the world of football card collecting.
