Billy &amp; Victoria Endgame Exes Reunite After Chelsea &amp; Nate Fall Short?

The Young and the Restless (Y&R) spoilers tease that Billy Abbott (Jason Thompson) and Victoria Newman (Amelia Heinle) could still be the end game, even though they are interested in dating other people.

Billy and Victoria are forever bonded through their children, Jonny Abbott (Paxton Mishkind) and Katie Abbott (Sienna Mercuri). Even though they are divorced, they remain in each other’s orbit to co-parent their children.

They have a pretty healthy relationship. They co-parent effectively and have remained close friends. Victoria has expressed reservations about Johnny developing a relationship with Chelsea Lawson (Melissa Claire Egan) but agreed with Billy that she would follow their son’s lead regarding his bio mom’s place in his life.

The Young And The Restless Spoilers– Victoria Newman’s Relationship With Nate Hastings Jr. Will Fail

Victoria seems infatuated with Nate Hastings, Jr (Sean Dominic). Last week, they brainstormed after hours with champagne and ended up in a steamy lip-lock. But Nate has a girlfriend he adores and wouldn’t want to lose.

The Young and the Restless spoilers say Nate will move mountains to keep Elena in his life. Not to mention, Victoria’s behavior with Nate is problematic. He’s her employee, and she gave him alcohol. Then she kissed him. That’s the legal definition of sexual harassment.

Nate wouldn’t sue Newman for sexual harassment, but Victoria should be careful and not cross the line. Another employee could report her for harassment if she’s not careful. Eventually, Nate will inform Victoria that he’s dedicated to Elena. He seems interested in Victoria, but the Y&R fans believe he admires her business skills more than having a romantic interest.

The Young And The Restless Updates – Billy Abbott’s Relationship With Chelsea Lawson Won’t Last

Billy has a thing for rescuing a damsel in distress. He sees Chelsea as a project, and once she’s better, he will lose interest. They have developed a close friendship, but their romantic interest in each other will eventually fizzle out.

The Young and the Restless spoilers revealed that Victoria was jealous of Lily Winters’ (Christel Khalil) relationship with Billy. The NE CEO said Lily got the mature Billy she always wanted. Instead, Victoria spent years trying to make their marriage work when Billy acted like an immature teenager.

The writers are preparing the Y & R audience for Billy and Victoria to share a steamy moment. They may end up together again one day.
