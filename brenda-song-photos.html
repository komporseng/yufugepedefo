Brenda Song Photos Of The Actress Hollywood Life

Brenda Song got started in showbusiness at a very early age. Her big break came when she was cast in London Tipton in the Disney Channel series ‘The Suite Life of Zack & Cody’ starring twins Cole and Dylan Sprouse. She starred in the series, as well as its spinoff and movie, and became a major Disney Channel star. Brenda also had notable roles over the years in Disney Channel Original Movies like ‘The Ultimate Christmas Present,’ ‘Stuck In The Suburbs,’ and ‘Get A Clue.’ She’s returning to Disney Channel for the new animated series ‘Amphibia,’ which premieres in June 2019. Brenda has also had recurring roles on ‘Scandal,’ ‘Station 19,’ and ‘New Girl.’ As of 2017, Brenda has been dating ‘Home Alone’ star Macauley Culkin.
