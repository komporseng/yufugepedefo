Tommy Vext – Biography

Tommy Vext was born in the United States on 15 April 1982. Lead vocalist for the bands Bad Wolves, Snot, Divine Heresy, and Westfield Rampage, who also formed the multi-platinum, heavy metal band, Five Finger Death Punch.

He stood in for Ivan L. Moody of Five Finger Death Punch during their 2017 European Arena Tour after Moody had checked into a medical facility. He performed at the local punk bands in New York City. He also competed in freestyle rap battles. In 1996, he founded a band called Vext.
