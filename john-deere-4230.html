John Deere 4230 Specs, Price, Review: The John Deere 4230 Tractor is part of the ‘Generation II’ series of tractors that the John Deere company produced many years ago.

John Deere 4230 Tractor fitted with John Deere diesel 6-cylinder liquid-cooled powerful engine, which delivers 86hp (64.1 kW) power.

John Deere 4230 tractor Attachments include Massey Ferguson 158 Loader and Massey Ferguson 148 High Lift Loader.

The fuel tank on the John Deere 4230 Tractor has a capacity of 37 US gallons (140 liters). It measures 104 inches (2.64 m) at its widest point.

The John Deere 4230 tractor weighs 10800 lbs (4898 kg) when operating normally, but when it’s fully ballasted, it weighs 14150 lbs (6418 kg), this is up from a shipping weight of just (3674 kg) if it has a Synro-Range transmission.
