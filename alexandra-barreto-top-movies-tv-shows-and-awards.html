Alexandra Barreto top movies, TV shows and awards

Alexandra Barreto is an American actress who appeared in the 2006 television series Pepper Dennis alongside her husband Rider Strong and Rebecca Romijn, as well as the 2007 horror film Tooth and Nail, which was part of the After Dark Horrorfest. Barreto has also played Ana Gutierrez, a recurring character on The Fosters.

Alexandra Barreto top movies and TV shows

Alexandra Barreto appeared in movies like All American, The Romanoffs, Mayans M.C, Pure Genius, The Fosters, Parenthood, Summerland, Sex, Love and Secrets etc.

Alexandra Barreto awards and nominations

Alexandra Barreto has not received an award yet, she was nominated Best Narrative Short (2019).

Alexandra, Rider Strong, and Shiloh Strong appeared in a television commercial for Barack Obama’s presidential campaign titled “It Could Happen To You” in 2008. The commercial was chosen as the funniest ad by MoveOn.org and aired on Comedy Central.

Barreto began dating actor Rider Strong after meeting on the set of the 2006 television series Pepper Dennis. They got engaged in December 2012 and married on October 20, 2013, in Oregon. Indigo “Indy” Barreto Strong, the couple’s only child, was born in December 2014.
